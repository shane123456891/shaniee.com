The Night Watch

The Night Watch is a 1642 painting by Dutch artist Rembrandt van Rijn. It is one of the most famous works of art from the Dutch Golden Age, depicting a group of militiamen in action. Known for its dramatic use of light and shadow, the painting captures a moment of movement and energy, making it one of Rembrandt's greatest masterpieces. It is currently displayed in the Rijksmuseum in Amsterdam, Netherlands.
