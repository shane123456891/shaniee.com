The Last Supper

The Last Supper is a late 15th-century mural painting by the Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci, one of the most famous works of art in the world. It depicts the moment Jesus Christ announces that one of his disciples will betray him, a pivotal scene in the Christian tradition. The painting is located in the Convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie in Milan, Italy. It has been displayed there since its completion in 1498 and continues to attract millions of visitors annually.

Attribute Details
Artist Leonardo da Vinci
Year 1495–1498
Medium Mural (tempera on gesso, pitch, and mastic)
Dimensions 460 cm × 880 cm (181 in × 346 in)
Location Convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie, Milan
