Mona Lisa

The Mona Lisa is a portrait painting by the Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci, considered one of the most famous and valuable artworks in the world.The Mona Lisa is located in the Louvre Museum in Paris, France. It has been on display there since 1797 and continues to attract millions of visitors each year. The painting is displayed in a climate-controlled, bulletproof glass case to protect it, making it a central feature of the museum’s collection.
